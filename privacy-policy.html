Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 21st September 2024

Introduction

At Cube27, ("us", "we", "our" or the "Company"), we value your privacy and understand the significance of safeguarding your data. This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") outlines our privacy practices for the activities detailed below. We aim to inform you about how we collect, store, access, and process information related to individuals, in line with your rights. In this Policy, personal data ("Personal Data") refers to any information that, either on its own or in combination with other available information, can identify an individual.

We are committed to safeguarding your privacy in compliance with the highest level of privacy regulations.

Scope

This policy applies to the website, services, products and apps offered by Cube27. However, this Policy does not extend to third-party products, services, websites or platforms that may be accessed through links provided by us (non-Cube27 links). These sites operate independently from us and have their own distinct privacy and data collection practices.

Any Personal Data that you provide to these websites will be subject to the third party's respective privacy policy. We cannot assume liability for the actions or policies of these independent sites, and we are not accountable for the content or privacy practices of such sites.

Definitions

  • "Client" means the processor or controller of the data with legitimate interest and valid consent of the data owners.
  • "Customer Data" means the personally identifiable information of the Data Subjects.
  • "Client Data" means data added to the Cube27 products, services, website and software applications using the client devices, technologies, softwares, tools, and any similar digital technology which originated from clients business environment.
  • "Applicable Laws" means application UK, European Union and US laws, including Federal, State and Local.

Terms

By using Cube27 services, website or applications the client agrees to abide by these Terms of Service.

The client acknowledges that:

  • They do not own the Customer Data, but do have the right to use the data subject to the terms defined in this agreement.
  • The client will control the access and processing of customer data to ensure that the access is provided to a limited authorised person.
  • The Client Data may be deleted, if the client terminates their commercial agreement.
  • The Client Data will be deleted within 30 days of the end of their contract term and may not be exported.

The Client will not use the data for:

  • Determining any person's employability, creditworthiness, credit standing, credit capacity or any other characteristics related to such a person's manner or mode of living.

Processing Activities

This Policy applies when you engage with us by performing any of the following:

Personal Data We Collect

When making a purchase or subscribing to our apps or services, Cube27 collects the following types of Personal Data:

  • Account Information: Includes your name, email address, phone number, physical address.
  • Feedback: Such as customer support or product reviews.

How We Collect Your Data

We gather your data including Personal Data from various sources:

  • From You: Provided when filling forms, using our services, corresponding via post, phone, email, or otherwise.
  • Automated technologies: Using cookies, server logs, and similar methods as you interact with our website.
  • Third parties: Receiving Personal Data from sources like other individuals, social media platforms, analytics providers, communication services, and providers of technical and delivery services.

Device and Usage Data

Cube27 automatically collects and stores information during website visits using cookies or similar technologies. This information includes Device IDs, log in details, IP Address, time stamps, authentication records, location, viewed products, web searches, time zone, and operational data.

Cookies

Cookies are small files containing information that your browser stores on your device. Information in these files is often shared with the site's owner and potential partners or third parties associated with that business. This information collection serves the functioning of the site and aims to enhance your experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • Strictly Necessary: Essential cookies are vital for the proper functioning of our web application.
  • Preference: We employ preference cookies to remember your preferred usage settings.
  • Personalization: Certain cookies personalize content to tailor your experience.
  • Analytics: We collect analytics to understand the demographics of site visitors, aiding us in enhancing our services and products.

Except for strictly necessary cookies, you have the option to opt in or out of cookie use at any time.

Retention, Deletion & Security

We will only retain your Personal Data for as long as necessary for the purpose for which that Personal data was collected and to the extent required by applicable law. When we no longer need your Personal Data, we will remove it from our systems and/or take steps to anonymise it.

  • Confidentiality: We will treat all your data as strictly confidential and will inform all its employees, agents and/or approved sub-processors involved in processing the data of the confidential nature of the data.
  • Security: We will implement technical and organizational measures to ensure security of the processing of your Personal Data.
  • Data Subject Requests: To the extent possible and commercially reasonable, we will assist you by appropriate measures to fulfill your obligation to respond to a data Subject request under applicable Data Protection Law.
  • Incident Management: If we become aware of a breach of security, we will notify you about the incident and take reasonable steps to prevent or mitigate the effects of the Security Incident.

How We Keep Your Data Safe

We have appropriate organizational safeguards and security measures in place to protect your Personal Data from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorized way, altered or disclosed. These include:

  • Security training to personnel and employees
  • Background verification of personnel
  • Network and system segmentation
  • Certificate-based security
  • Sensitive data encryption during transition and at rest
  • Secure key management policies and procedures
  • Separation of staging and production environments

Cube27 uses Google and other third-party tools, services and platforms to store your information securely. The communication between your browser and our website uses a secure encrypted connection wherever your Personal Data is involved.

Your Rights for Your Personal Data

Depending on your geographical location and citizenship, your rights concerning data privacy are subject to local data protection regulations. These rights may encompass:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to confirm if we are processing your Personal Data and request a copy of the Personal Data held about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to rectify incomplete or inaccurate Personal Data we hold about you.
  • Right to be Forgotten: You can request the deletion of Personal Data we hold, unless its retention is necessary due to legal obligations or legitimate interests.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: Under certain circumstances, you can restrict our processing of your Personal Data.
  • Right to Portability: You can receive your Personal Data we store in a structured, electronic format.
  • Right to Opt Out: You have the right to opt out of specific Personal Data processing purposes like targeted advertising.
  • Right to Objection: If our processing is based on legitimate interests, you can object unless we have compelling grounds for processing.
  • Non-discrimination: Exercising your rights should not result in service denial or altered experiences.
  • File a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent data protection authority.
  • Withdrawing Consent: You can withdraw consent for processing your Personal Data at any time.
