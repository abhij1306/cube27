Cube27 specializes in delivering bespoke technology solutions with a focus on MarTech, eCommerce, and Business Intelligence (BI) & Analytics. Their service portfolio is structured to support digital transformations through a Global Capability Center (GCC) model, encompassing everything from rapid product development to complex enterprise migrations.
The primary service areas and specializations include:
1. Integrated Digital Services
Cube27 categorizes its digital expertise into six core pillars aimed at comprehensive process and product enhancement:
• eCommerce Solutions
• AI & Rapid Product Development
• Engineering & Development Services
• Cloud & DevOps
• Analytics & AI
• Process Automation
2. eCommerce & CMS Platforms
The company segments its eCommerce offerings into two tracks to cater to different business scales:
• Enterprise Platforms: Implementation and support for high-level systems, specifically Salesforce Commerce Cloud (Demandware), SAP Commerce Cloud (Hybris), and Magento Commerce.
• OpenSource Platforms: Deployment of agile solutions including WooCommerce, Shopify, Ubercart, PrestaShop, and Joomla.
• Core Services: Beyond platform selection, they provide architecture, deployment, custom CMS development (JAVA, .NET, PHP), and complex migrations, having completed over 30 implementations including work for Fortune 500 clients.
3. Marketing Technology (MarTech)
Cube27 facilitates data connectivity and campaign optimization through:
• Integrations: Connecting systems across analytics (Google Analytics, Tealium), APIs (Adwords, Bing, Facebook), and CRMs (Oracle Eloqua, Adobe Marketing Cloud).
• Optimization: Services include Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) and multivariate A/B testing.
• Proprietary Tools: They utilize internal solutions such as DSMM Advantage for campaign management and BI reporting, and AQ360, an award-winning dynamic call tracking solution.
4. Business Intelligence & Analytics
Their BI capabilities are built on a comprehensive technical stack designed to handle data from extraction to advanced analysis:
• Visualization: Using tools like Tableau, PowerBI, and Google Data Studio.
• Data Infrastructure:
    ◦ ETL: Microsoft SSIS, CozyRoc, Clover ETL, and AWS Lambda.
    ◦ Warehousing: Snowflake, Google Big Query, and Amazon Redshift.
    ◦ Cloud Storage: Amazon S3 and Google Cloud storage.
• Advanced Analysis: Leveraging R libraries, Rapidminer, and Machine Learning.
5. Web & Mobile Architecture
The company supports modern application development across various verticals such as Telecom, Education, and Health.
• Web Technologies: Usage of frameworks like Angular, ReactJS, Node JS, PHP, and .NET.
• Mobile Development: Specialization in Flutter and React Native, as well as the creation of Progressive Web Apps (PWA) and Accelerated Mobile Pages (AMP)
