Establishing a High-Performance India Operation for a Global Feed Technology Company

A leading feed management platform required rapid expansion of their operational capabilities while ensuring the new team maintained specific technical quality and cultural alignment with their US headquarters.

The Challenge

The client needed to build a dedicated India team quickly but couldn't compromise on technical quality or cultural fit. Traditional outsourcing models had failed to deliver the integration they needed—the team felt like vendors, not colleagues.

The Solution

  • GCC Setup: Cube27 initiated a "Build-Operate" model, handling the legal entity setup, infrastructure, and compliance.
  • Stringent Hiring: Executed a rigorous recruitment drive screening for both technical aptitude in data handling and cultural fit.
  • Culture Mirroring: Implemented HR operations that mirrored the client's internal values, ensuring the India team operated as a seamless extension.

End-to-End PO, Challan, & Invoice System for Manufacturing Suppliers

A major supplier faced critical inefficiencies in managing the Purchase Order (PO) to Payment lifecycle. Manual Excel-based invoicing led to errors and made historical data analysis impossible.

The Challenge

The client was generating documents without database tracking. Every invoice was manually created in Excel, leading to data inconsistencies, duplicate entries, and zero visibility into historical patterns or pricing trends.

The Solution

  • Custom Development: Built a bespoke web-based ERP system using Angular frontend and Node.js backend.
  • Workflow Automation: Created a "PO → Challan → Invoice → SRV" workflow. The system automatically converts POs into Challans and subsequently into Invoices.
  • MAT COD Integration: Integrated specific material code columns in the invoice module to align with industry-standard Excel formats.
  • Historical Intelligence: Built a data warehouse layer for analyzing historical trends, pricing history, and volume metrics instantly.
